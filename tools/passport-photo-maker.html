How to Use Passport Photo Maker

Upload your headshot, choose a country preset (or custom size in millimeters), and use the Zoom slider plus drag-to-position to fit your face inside the frame. Adjust brightness, contrast, and saturation (tone) to get a clear, printable photo. Select export DPI (300 DPI recommended) and click Generate → Download. The tool exports a correctly sized image in pixels computed from the selected DPI and mm size.

Top Benefits

  • Privacy: All processing happens in your browser. No files are uploaded.
  • Accurate sizing: Export pixel-perfect images based on chosen mm and DPI.
  • Simple editing: Pan, zoom, crop, and adjust image tone quickly.
  • Multiple presets: Presets for UK, USA, Australia and custom sizes.
  • Fast and free: No signup, works on desktop and modern mobile browsers.
