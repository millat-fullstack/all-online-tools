What is WebP Format?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google. It provides superior compression without losing quality, which makes websites load faster. However, not every platform or editing software supports WebP. That’s why many users prefer to convert WebP to JPG for universal use.

Why Convert WebP to JPG?

JPG is the most widely used image format globally. Almost every device, application, and website supports it. By using a WebP to JPG converter, you ensure that your images open correctly everywhere, whether for email, social media, or print.
