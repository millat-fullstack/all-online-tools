Why Convert PNG to JPG?

PNG images are great for transparency and sharp quality, but they often come with larger file sizes. JPG images, on the other hand, are more compact and widely supported on all platforms and devices. By converting PNG to JPG, you save storage space, improve loading times, and ensure better compatibility with websites, social media platforms, and email attachments.
