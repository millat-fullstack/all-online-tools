How Does OCR Work?

OCR scans your image, detects letters, and converts image to text that you can copy, edit, or save as a Word file. The process uses advanced AI models to improve accuracy, even with low-quality images. Whether you paste a screenshot or upload a file, the tool extracts text instantly.
